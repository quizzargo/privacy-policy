Privacy Policy — Quizzar Go

Last updated: November 4, 2025

Quizzar Go ("we", "us", or "the App") is a learning and quiz-generation app that uses trusted AI technologies to make studying and revision easier. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Quizzar Go mobile application.

Summary

Short version: Quizzar Go does not sell or share your personal data. The app sends user prompts and optional images to trusted AI services (Google Gemini and OpenAI ChatGPT) to generate quiz content. No personal information or images are stored by us. Technical device data is collected via Firebase for analytics and security. Ads and rewards are powered by Google AdMob.

Information We Collect

1. User-provided information

When you type prompts, quiz titles, or chat messages, that text is sent securely to AI services to generate content such as multiple-choice questions, fill-in-the-blanks, or explanations. If you use the camera feature, captured images are sent only to the AI provider for processing and are not stored, uploaded, or saved by us.

2. Automatically collected information

The app may collect limited technical data through Firebase services, including:

  • Crash reports and basic diagnostics (via Firebase Crashlytics — optional)
  • Anonymous usage data (Firebase Analytics — optional)
  • Device details for app usage tracking:
await _firestore.collection('app_users').add({
  'device_type': deviceType,
  'timestamp': now,
  'platform': Platform.operatingSystem,
});

This data helps improve stability and can be deleted upon request.

3. Local storage

Your quizzes, tokens, and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure storage (Hive or SharedPreferences). The app does not access your photos, media, contacts, or external files.

How We Use Information

  • To generate quizzes and AI-based educational content
  • To store user preferences and quiz progress locally
  • To analyze performance and fix bugs
  • To deliver rewarded ads and manage the qToken economy (used to unlock AI features)

Accuracy Disclaimer

AI-generated content may contain inaccuracies. Quizzar Go does not guarantee that all questions or answers are factually correct. Users should not rely solely on generated material for examinations or critical decisions. We strongly advise not to include personal or sensitive information in prompts or chat inputs.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to deliver core features:

  • AI Providers: Google Gemini and OpenAI — for AI-generated quizzes and explanations.
  • Firebase: Analytics, Crashlytics, Firestore, and Remote Config — for app diagnostics and configuration.
  • Ad Networks: Google AdMob — for showing ads and rewarded videos.

When ads are displayed, AdMob may collect anonymous identifiers and usage data per their Privacy Policy.

Open Source & Licensed Assets

Quizzar Go uses open-licensed design and animation assets:

  • Lucide Icons — Open-source icon set under the ISC License
  • Poppins Font — Google Fonts under the SIL Open Font License
  • Lottie Animations — Licensed under the Lottie Simple License (e.g., “Confetti” by Mario Cabrera)

These assets are for visual enhancement only and do not collect or transmit user data.

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it. Firebase data (e.g., crash logs) follows Google’s retention policies. You may contact us to request deletion of any Firebase entries associated with your device.

Children

Quizzar Go is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us for immediate removal.

Security

We use HTTPS encryption and trusted APIs to handle data securely. However, no system is completely secure, and we recommend avoiding sensitive personal details in prompts or uploads.

Your Choices

  • You can clear all app data at any time from your device settings.
  • You can disable ads personalization via Google’s ad settings.
  • You can request deletion of your anonymous Firebase data by emailing us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when the app’s data handling or third-party services change. The "Last Updated" date above will always reflect the latest version.
